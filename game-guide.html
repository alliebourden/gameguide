Discover Current Hot Games

Discover a curated collection of the most recent and trending board games, with the option to conveniently sort them by name or their year of release. Navigate through the list using the Previous and Next buttons to access more game options. When you find a game that catches your attention, just click on "Learn More" to access in-depth information about that specific game. You can also express your interest by clicking the heart icon to 'favorite' games while browsing, and easily review all your favourite games on the Liked Games page.
